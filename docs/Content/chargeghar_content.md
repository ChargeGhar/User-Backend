## Renting Policy
**URL:** `/renting-policy/`  
**Last Updated:** February 2026

### 1. Rental Process

| Step | Title | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Find a Station | Open the app and use the live map to locate the nearest available station. |
| 2 | Scan the QR Code | Tap "Rent", scan the QR code. Ensure sufficient wallet balance. |
| 3 | Collect Your Power Bank | Slot opens and power bank is released. Rental timer begins immediately. |
| 4 | Charge Your Device | Use built-in cables (USB-C, Lightning, Micro-USB). Power bank is portable. |
| 5 | Return to Any Station | Insert into any available slot at any Charge Ghar station. LED turns 🟢 green on success. Check app for "Rental Ended" notification. |

### 2. Fees & Charges

| Fee Type | Amount | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Base Fee (First Hour) | NPR [Base Fee] | Charged from moment power bank is released. |
| Hourly Rate | NPR [Hourly Rate] | Per hour after the first hour. |
| Daily Cap (24 hrs) | NPR [Daily Cap] | Maximum charge per 24-hour period. |
| Security Deposit | NPR [Deposit] | Refundable upon successful return. |
| Overdue Fee | NPR [Overdue Rate] | Applied after maximum rental period is exceeded. |
| Lost Penalty | NPR [Lost Penalty] | Charged if not returned within [X] days. |

> 💡 Actual fee amounts are displayed in the app before confirming rental. Charges auto-deducted from Charge Ghar wallet.

### 3. Overdue & Non-Return Policy
- Power bank considered **Overdue** after **[X] hours** without return.
- Push notifications sent before overdue status is triggered.
- Overdue fees accumulate on top of regular rental fees.
- Marked as **Lost** if not returned within **[X] days**.
- Lost penalty of **NPR [Penalty Amount]** charged immediately.
- Security deposit forfeited on non-return.
- Accounts suspended until Lost penalty is paid.
- Returning after Lost marking may result in partial penalty refund at Charge Ghar's discretion.

### 4. Returning the Power Bank
- Return to **any available slot at any Charge Ghar station** across Nepal.
- Insert firmly until it clicks into place.
- Slot LED turns 🟢 **Green** to confirm successful return.
- In-app "Rental Ended" notification with final bill summary.
- If slot full or technical error, try another slot or nearby station.
- Do not leave power bank unattended without completing digital return in app.
- Security deposit refunded to wallet within **24 hours** of confirmed return.

### 5. Power Bank Care
- Avoid dropping, getting wet, or exposing to extreme temperatures.
- Do not use visibly damaged power banks — report via app's Help section.
- Do not open, disassemble, or modify the power bank.
- Intentional damage results in full replacement cost charge.

### 6. Cancellation
- Rental cannot be cancelled once power bank is released from station.
- To end rental, return the power bank to any Charge Ghar station.
- Accidental rental due to technical error: contact support within **5 minutes** with a screenshot.
